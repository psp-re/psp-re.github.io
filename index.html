Welcome

Welcome to the PSP Reverse Engineering Headquarters. Here you'll find all kinds of helpful tools, tips, and blog posts about Reverse Engineering the Sony Playstation Portable game console. It is run by @sajattack and his friends from the PSP Homebrew Discord Server.
